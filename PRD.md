# PRD.md — Vilunim Marketing Website

## Product Overview
Vilunim is an AI voice receptionist service for New Zealand trade businesses. The website is the primary sales asset — it educates prospects on the problem (missed calls = lost revenue) and converts them into booked demo calls.

**Status**: MVP build  
**URL**: vilunim.com  
**Owner**: Tui Levita (Director)

---

## Problem We're Solving
NZ tradies (plumbers, electricians, builders, HVAC, etc.) miss calls constantly while on the tools. Every missed call is a potential job lost to a competitor. They can't hire a receptionist — it's too expensive and unnecessary at their scale.

**Core pain**: Tradies are too busy working to answer the phone, but unanswered phones cost them money.

---

## Solution
Vilunim provides Lydia — an AI voice receptionist that:
- Answers every call, 24/7
- Qualifies leads with natural conversation
- Captures job details and contact info
- Routes urgent calls appropriately
- Delivers lead summaries to the tradie

No missed calls. No lost jobs. No staff needed.

---

## Target User
**Primary**: NZ trade business owners — plumbers, electricians, builders, HVAC technicians  
**Profile**: Owner-operator, 1–5 employees, $300k–$2M revenue, always on the tools  
**Pain level**: High — they know missed calls cost them but have no good solution  
**Tech comfort**: Low-medium — needs a simple, human explanation of the product

---

## Website Goals
1. **Educate** — Help the tradie understand the missed-call problem and that a solution exists
2. **Build trust** — Make Vilunim feel credible, professional, and NZ-local
3. **Convert** — Get them to book a 20-minute demo call

**Primary CTA**: "Book a Free Demo"  
**Secondary CTA**: "See How It Works"
